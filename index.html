Welcome to Telcowise Solutions

Telcowise Solutions is a Texas-based telecom drafting firm that supports engineering and construction workflows nationwide. We help streamline projects for A&E firms, contractors, utilities, and municipalities by delivering fast, accurate, and professional construction documentation. Our mission is to remove bottlenecks in production, improve clarity in design, and make your job easier.

What We Do

We produce highly detailed drawings for wireless infrastructure, fiber overlays, zoning submittals, permitting, traffic control plans, and more. Every drawing is checked for compliance and accuracy, ensuring that your projects move smoothly from conception to approval. We also accommodate specific templates, CAD standards, and file delivery methods—tailored to your organization’s needs.

Who We Serve

Our clients include wireless telecom carriers, A&E design firms, permitting specialists, fiber providers, and construction contractors. Whether it’s a large-scale deployment in a metropolitan area or a rural modification, we adjust our workflow and provide timely, consistent drafting support that integrates seamlessly into your pipeline.

How We Work

We work remotely with clients across the U.S., providing a flexible and scalable drafting team. Our work ethic is built on responsiveness, attention to detail, and reliability. We maintain organized project folders, adapt to your standards, and deliver on time—even with tight schedules. Communication is always clear and prompt.

Why Telcowise?

Because we understand what you need. From redlines and design updates to full construction sets and permitting packages, we cover it all. We’ve worked in a wide range of jurisdictions, from strict Caltrans TCP requirements in California to more flexible zoning in the Midwest. Telcowise is your drafting partner, whether for ongoing programs or urgent one-off jobs.
